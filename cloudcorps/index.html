Due to a rapid increase in Litecoin mining difficulty, Cloud Corps' original business plan is no longer feasible. It was a grand experiment and wonderful adventure, and to everyone who made it possible:

Thanks for believing in us!

About

Cloud Corps was a charitable supercomputing network. Pick a charity, download an app, and put your computer to work. We'll donate 95% of your computer's earnings to the charity you selected.

Code

All of our code has been open sourced. We owe an immeasurable debt to the open source community, and this was the least we could do to give back.
